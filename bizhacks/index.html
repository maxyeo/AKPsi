What is BizHacks?

A unique opportunity to meet AKPsi brothers from other chapters while showing off your innovation skills in an elevator pitch competition. Brothers join as individuals and we will create teams of AKPsi brothers from different chapters and with different skills. As a team, you will brainstorm and pitch a business idea together to entrepreneurs and industry professionals!
