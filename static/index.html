About Rho Psi

The Rho Psi chapter at JHU focuses its activities on professional development and brotherhood. Brothers of AKPsi are among the best students interested in business and are involved in the campus and the community. AKPsi organizes many business-related networking and speaker events during the academic year for Hopkins undergraduates, include the Elevator Pitch Competition (EPC) where students have 90 seconds to present business ideas to industry leaders


Founded - 2001 at Johns Hopkins University

Countries - Our brothers represent 10 different countries

Majors - Our brothers represent 20 different majors, ranging from International Studies to Biomedical Engineering to Neuroscience and everything in between.

About Alpha Kappa Psi

Alpha Kappa Psi is the oldest and largest professional business fraternity. Its alumni have held almost every major political position, including the presidency and vice presidency. Alumni have held notable positions on board of directors and executive management teams of some of the world's most well-known companies.


Founded - 1904 at New York University

Membership - Nearly 240,000 members initiated worldwide.

Chapters - Chartered at more than 300 chapters in the United States, Canada and the United Kingdom

Vision Statement - Alpha Kappa Psi is recognized as a premier developer in principled business leaders.
